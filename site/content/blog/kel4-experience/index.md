I have been to six osu! LAN events as of March 2026. But this one, KEL4 in Gdańsk, was my favourite by far.

There’s something so special about travelling to another country and finding yourself surrounded by people who share this amazing common ground with you. At this point in my life, I don’t think I could’ve asked for a meetup more meaningful. It’s hard to put into words how much it all meant to me.

From the moment we all arrived, everything just felt right. Whether we were playing pool, out bowling, or just sitting around talking, I felt a kind of joy that doesn’t come around very often. The kind where you’re fully present, not thinking about anything else, just appreciating where you are and who you’re with.

What stood out most to me wasn’t the event itself, but the people who I spent time with. Especially the time spent at the goon bnb, the eight of us sharing that space, those late night conversations were really cool. It’s strange how quickly something can start to feel important when you're surrounded by the right people.

There was a level of comfort and connection that I don’t take for granted. Being able to talk openly, laugh freely, and just let loose without overthinking, it meant everything to me. Those small, seemingly insignificant moments ended up being the ones I’ll remember the most.

This event has reinstagnated why I play osu! in the first place. It’s easy to think of osu! as just a game, or the time spent grinding it as something intangible, but being here, with you guys, it makes playing feel worthwhile. When you find the right people, osu! has a really cool community.

To everyone who was there, especially those of us at the goon bnb, thank you, love you.
